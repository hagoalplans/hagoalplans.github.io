Candy Goal ("we", "our", or "us") is a casual candy matching puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Game Data

All game progress (levels, coins, achievements) is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or store this data on any server.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads. AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to personalize ads.

Children's Privacy

Our game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Since game data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it.
